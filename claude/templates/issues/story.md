## Overview

**As a:** <Insert user persona, stakeholder, person.
_Our team should have a shared understanding of who this
persona is. We understand how that person works, how they
think and what they feel. We have empathy for them>_

**I want:** _<Here we're describing their intent, not the
features they use. What is it they're actually trying to
achieve? This statement should be implementation free_>

**So that:** _<How does their immediate desire to do
something this fit into their bigger picture? What's the
overall benefit they're trying to achieve? What is the big
problem that needs solving?>_
